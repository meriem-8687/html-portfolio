My top 3 movies of all time

The Maze Runner

*The Maze Runner* is a thrilling dystopian adventure where a group of teens must navigate a deadly, ever-changing maze while uncovering secrets about their mysterious captivity and the world beyond.

The Matrix

*The Matrix* is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller where a hacker discovers that reality is a simulated illusion controlled by machines, leading him to fight for humanity's freedom in a high-stakes digital rebellion.

Shutter Island

*Shutter Island* is a psychological thriller where a U.S. Marshal unravels dark secrets and confronts his own reality while investigating the mysterious disappearance of a patient from a remote psychiatric facility.
